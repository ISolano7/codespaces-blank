Our Story

BORN ON THE BORDER.
RAISED ON THE RANCH.

LOCA YOLK is the wildly delicious collision of American breakfast culture and the soulful flavors of Mexico — slow-cooked salsas, hand-pressed tortillas, local eggs, and enough chile heat to wake the dead.

We open at sunrise and close when the last plate is licked clean. Every dish tells a story. Every bite is a border crossing.
